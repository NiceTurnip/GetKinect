Kinect's mobile phone access system allows gym members to securely enter the facility using their smartphones, eliminating the need for traditional gym key fobs or cards. After registering through the Kinect gym software, members use their phone to unlock the gym door, but only when they are physically present at the facility.

The system leverages Wi-Fi and geolocation to verify that the member is at the location, preventing any remote or unauthorized access.

This 24/7 gym access control system ensures a smooth, secure, and efficient entry process for both members and gym owners. It reduces staffing needs and enhances convenience, making it ideal for gyms that operate with minimal staff or around the clock.
