Access Control

Give your members a modern, seamless entry experience with smartphone-based door access. Set custom access schedules, manage permissions in real time, and monitor entry activity with detailed logs. No key fobs, no clunky hardware—just full control, 24/7.

  • Smartphone-based door entry
  • Custom access schedules & permissions
  • Real-time access logs & activity tracking

Membership Management

From individual memberships to family accounts and shared contracts, our platform makes it easy to manage every member relationship. Create digital waivers, update profiles, control access levels, and keep your entire member base organized in one place.

  • Member database with profile & access control
  • Shared/family accounts & digital waivers
  • Online registration and easy onboarding

Scheduling

Simplify your calendar with flexible scheduling tools built for busy gyms. Manage classes, workshops, and appointments with support for bookings, waitlists, and check-ins. Members can reserve a spot from their phone, while you stay in control of capacity and timing.

  • Class & event booking with waitlist support
  • Member check-ins and attendance tracking
  • Real-time updates and capacity management

Billing

Automate your gym's finances with secure, built-in billing tools. Set up recurring memberships, send one-time invoices, and track payments without lifting a finger. With fully integrated payment processing, your revenue flows smoothly—and so does your business.

  • Automated recurring billing & one-time invoices
  • Integrated payment processing
  • Free trials and membership upgrades

Reporting

Make smarter decisions with real-time reporting that tracks everything from member activity to class attendance and revenue trends. Whether you're managing day-to-day operations or planning for growth, our insights help you stay informed and in control.

  • Attendance & check-in analytics
  • Revenue and billing reports
  • Member engagement insights
